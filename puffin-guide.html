Why Poppins for Puffin

Poppins is a geometric sansâ€‘serif typeface from Google Fonts known for its clean, friendly shapes and excellent readability from small to large sizes. Its modern forms align with our Puffin brand: approachable like the bird, yet confident and professional for an educational platform.

  • Clarity for learning: Open counters and balanced proportions aid onâ€‘screen reading for students and educators.
  • Warmth with structure: Rounded terminals soften the geometry, striking a welcoming tone for schools.
  • International reach: Broad weight range and wide language coverage make it suitable for a global audience.
  • Consistency: One font family across headings, UI, and body ensures a unified brand voice.
  • Accessibility: Great legibility supports inclusive design and WCAGâ€‘aligned contrast choices.
