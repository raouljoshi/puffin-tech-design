Privacy Policy

How Puffin protects your data and maintains your privacy

Last updated: August 13, 2025

TL;DR: Puffin is privacy‑first. We process your audio securely to create a transcript and draft, but do not retain audio. We minimize data (student first name, grade band, language), process in the EU, and you decide what to keep, share, or delete.

1) What we process (data minimization)

  • Account: Email for sign‑in and service messages. User name and language for profile configuration.
  • Student context: First name, grade band (1–4, 5–8, 9–12), output language.
  • Audio: Used to generate a transcript/draft - not stored.
  • Generated content: Transcripts and drafts you choose to save.

Please avoid entering sensitive personal data. Children’s full identifiers are not required and should not be added.

2) How we use data (purpose & lawful basis)

  • Purpose: Provide the voice‑to‑feedback service, improve quality, ensure security.
  • Lawful basis (direct use): Legitimate interests (providing an educator tool) balanced with your rights.
  • Lawful basis (school‑contracted use): We act as a processor for the school (controller).
  • No marketing or ads: We do not sell data or share it for advertising.

3) Storage, sharing & retention

  • Audio: Deleted after processing.
  • Transcripts/drafts: Stored until you delete them; schools may define retention when they are the controller.
  • Access: By default, only the creating teacher can view entries. Future optional sharing with school staff will be opt‑in and granular.
  • Sub‑processors: We use vetted EU data‑region providers under contract and with Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

4) Security & location

  • EU processing: Data processed and stored in the EU.
  • Encryption: In transit and at rest; least‑privilege access controls; audit logging.
  • Cookies/analytics: Only essential cookies; privacy‑preserving analytics where used.
